Our History

Metadise Academy was established as part of Metadise’s mission to support talent development in Sarawak and Malaysia through practical, industry-focused education.

The academy was created to help students, professionals, and organizations gain relevant digital skills in areas such as technology, artificial intelligence, creative media, and professional development.

Through hands-on training, real-world learning experiences, and industry collaboration, Metadise Academy continues to provide learning opportunities that prepare individuals for the demands of the modern digital economy.

Our Vision

To become a trusted learning academy that develops future-ready talents through technology, creativity, innovation, and industry-driven education.

Our Mission

To provide practical and accessible learning experiences that empower individuals and organizations with the skills needed to grow, adapt, and succeed in the digital economy.
